OSINT tips for job candidates

This article covers how to OSINT yourself, why an employer might do it, and what they would look at.

  1. Employers may look into your criminal history, address, phone number, email address, education, work experience, skills, social media profiles, online resume, LinkedIn profile, credit report, driving record, and online activity.
  2. You can use OSINT to check your own footprint outside of Googling your name. There are a number of tools and suggestions available to help you do this.
  3. It is important to be aware of the background data that employers may be interested in so that you can take steps to protect your privacy and manage your online reputation.
