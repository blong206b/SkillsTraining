Skills to Prioritize

Here are the key skills to prioritize for the Per Scholas job opportunity and your broader career interests in cybersecurity, data analysis, and web development:

  • CySA+ Certification (003 Version): Upgrade to the latest version of CySA+ certification.
  • Canvas LMS Proficiency: Gain proficiency in using Canvas for course management and online instruction.
  • Cybersecurity Knowledge: Stay updated with the latest trends and practices in cybersecurity.
  • Teaching and Instructional Design: Enhance skills in instructional design and effective teaching methodologies.
  • Project Management: Develop or refine project management skills for course content and schedules.
  • Online Presentation Skills: Improve ability to present and engage with students in virtual settings.
  • Learning Assessment: Explore techniques for designing and conducting assessments in online courses.
  • Technical Writing: Sharpen technical writing skills for course materials.
  • Professional Development: Stay engaged with professional development opportunities.
  • Communication and Soft Skills: Refine communication and soft skills for effective online teaching.
  • Canvas LMS Administration (Optional): Gain proficiency in Canvas LMS administration tasks.
  • Content Creation Tools: Familiarize with tools for creating engaging online content.
  • Accessibility and Inclusivity Training: Understand best practices for making online courses accessible to all learners.
  • Data Analysis and Web Development (Optional): Upgrade skills in data analysis and web development technologies.

Starting with the CySA+ certification and Canvas LMS proficiency is a strong foundation for the Per Scholas role. The other skills listed will help you excel in your role as a Learning and Development Associate and align with your broader career objectives in the tech industry. Remember to set clear learning goals and timelines for acquiring or upgrading these skills.
